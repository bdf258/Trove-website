Make the world a library

Illiteracy is one of the UK's most persistent social problems. 16% of UK adults are functionally illiterate, and that number has not changed in 20 years. Our research suggests it comes down to access to books.

We have a chance to change this. By hosting a trove, you become part of a network that turns the world into a library - and the profits go to building troves in schools and food banks.
