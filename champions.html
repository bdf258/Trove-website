Help us build a distributed library

We cannot do this without you. Luckily, this is where our interests align: the more books everyone scans, the more everyone has access to. Picture this: you want something out of a specific selection of books (a goodreads list, maybe?) and are willing to go round local charity shops to find it. Scanning books in with our computer vision tool won't take much longer than doing it alone - and everyone else then has access to that data. Including you, from last time anyone went around.

Sound good? Join us and we'll make the world a library.
