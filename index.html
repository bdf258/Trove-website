A Social Problem

16% of UK adults are functionally illiterate, and much of that comes from a lack of access to books. A third of households have fewer than 20 books while library loans have almost halved in a decade.

A Social Solution

Sharing economies never quite took off, which is why we've designed Trove by learning from these failures. We've ensured - and tested - that all parties have clear economic gains from participating.

A Centre of the Community

Independent cafes often struggle to gain new customers, especially when they're out of the way. Trove gives an easy way to invite more people into the shop.
